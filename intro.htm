I was born and raised in Seoul, South Korea. My world didn’t open yet until I got to know about Distance Learning.

Distance Learning

It was a turning point of my life when I started learning throughout Internet. My inflexible schedule with two young babies and inadequate financial situation were always my cliche excuses to put off from learning at school. I realized that learning maybe easier and closer to me than I ever thought when I introduced my kids math website to learn online. I started learning English and Technology throughout Internet and I am still taking coding courses at Massive open online courses(MOOCs), which offer large-scale interactive participation and open access through the World Wide Web or other network technologies.

Distance Learning opens the way to see the word for me. And now, I hope I can inspire people who are eager to learn just like I was.
